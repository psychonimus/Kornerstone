Why Kornerstone

About Us

Kornerstone is a boutique consultancy at the forefront of Application Lifecycle Management (ALM) and Robotic Process Automation (RPA) solutions tailored for SAP and Salesforce ecosystems.

We combine deep expertise in enterprise technologies with a passion for innovation to empower organizations in optimizing their digital operations and achieving transformative outcomes.

Our mission is to streamline business processes, enhance operational efficiency, and drive digital transformation. Leveraging advanced technologies such as SAP Cloud ALM, we offer comprehensive application lifecycle management to ensure seamless integration and optimal performance of enterprise applications.

Vision

To Revolutionize Enterprise Automation by Creating a Unified Framework that drives Efficiency, Adaptability, and Long-term Success.

Mission

To Empower Businesses by Delivering Scalable and Robust Test Automation and RPA solutions, establishing a Sustainable Foundation for Seamless Integration, Continuous Improvement, and developing a Unified Automation Platform.
