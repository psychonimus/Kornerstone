Job Description

We are looking for an enthusiastic Presales Consultant to join our team in Pune. The ideal candidate will have 1-2 years of experience in presales or a related field, with the ability to support the sales team through technical expertise and strong communication skills.

  • Job Title: Presales Consultant
  • Location: Pune, India
  • Experience: 1-2 Years

Responsibilities:

  • Work closely with the sales team to understand customer requirements and provide technical solutions.
  • Prepare and deliver compelling product demonstrations and presentations.
  • Assist in the preparation of proposals, RFPs, and other sales documents.
  • Conduct market research to understand industry trends and customer needs.
  • Support the sales team in identifying and qualifying potential opportunities.
  • Collaborate with product development teams to ensure alignment with customer requirements.
  • Provide technical support and guidance to customers during the sales process.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Business, or a related field.
  • 1-2 years of experience in presales, technical consulting, or a related role.
  • Strong technical acumen and understanding of software solutions.
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills.
  • Ability to understand and explain complex technical concepts.
  • Strong organizational and time management skills.
  • Proficiency in MS Office Suite and CRM software.

Preferred

  • Experience in [IT/ITES].
  • Knowledge of the local market and customer base.
