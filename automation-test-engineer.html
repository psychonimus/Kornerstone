Automation Test Engineer

Job Description

We are looking for a skilled Automation Test Engineer to join our team in Pune. The ideal candidate will have 2-3 years of experience in developing and executing automated tests to ensure the quality of our software products.

  • Job Title: Automation Test Engineer
  • Location: Pune, India
  • Experience: 2-3 Years

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and maintain automated test scripts for web and mobile applications.
  • Execute automated tests and analyze results to identify issues..
  • Collaborate with the development team to understand requirements and improve test coverage.
  • Integrate automated tests into the CI/CD pipeline.
  • Report and track defects, ensuring resolution before release.
  • Continuously improve test automation frameworks and tools.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, IT, or related field.
  • 2-3 years of experience in automation testing.
  • Proficiency in automation tools like Selenium, Appium, or similar.
  • Strong programming skills in languages such as Java, Python, or JavaScript.
  • Experience with CI/CD tools (e.g., Jenkins, GitLab).
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Good communication and teamwork skills.

Preferred

  • Experience with performance testing tools (e.g. JMeter).
  • Knowledge of BDD/TDD practices.
  • ISTQB certification or equivalent.
